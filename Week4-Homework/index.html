Ehh, What's up, Doc?

Providing the right care for your bunnies is essential for their well-being. Follow these tips to ensure a happy and healthy life for your furry friends.

Bunny Care Tips

  • Provide a balanced diet with fresh hay, vegetables, and pellets.
  • Ensure access to clean and fresh water at all times.
  • Create a safe and SPACIOUS living environment for your bunnies.

Creating the Perfect Bunny Habitat

Design a comfortable and secure living space for your bunnies. Consider the following when setting up their habitat:

  1. Use a spacious cage or pen with room for hopping and exploration.
  2. Provide soft bedding material for comfort.
  3. Add hiding spots and toys for mental stimulation.
